Milestone: Two

Site Planning Analysis Page

Web Site Goal - describe the goal of your web site in one or two sentences.

Encourage conversation about our current energy options and their effects on our environment. Make the topic personal by using a fictional scenario / thought experiment and surveying the results.

What results do you want to see?

I would like to see how people choose when the subject is made personal.

List the working title of each page on your website.

  1. Home
  2. Natural Gas
  3. Coal
  4. Nuclear
  5. Wind
  6. Solar
  7. Survey (form)

Indicate what type of data you will ask the user to enter into the form.

The users will be asked to rank the choices in order from most to least desired after reviewing the material.

What information do you need?

  • Graphics: I will generate them from Tinkercad.
  • Facts: Basic usage facts, descriptions, categories and current rankings by usage from referenced websites.
  • Text: The basic premise of the scenario / thought experiment with guidelines will be self generated.
  • Form: I hope to make the form myself, but I might find a pre-made template.
