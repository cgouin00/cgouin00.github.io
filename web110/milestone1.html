Topic Approval

What is the purpose of the site?
Elevate an often overlooked topic worthy of discussion back into the public arena. Additionaly, I hope to raise awareness about energy alternatives' respective pro's, con's and their impact on the environment.
What do you want the web site to accomplish?
Conducting an informal survey using source material followed by a form that asks users to rank preferred sources (in a theoretical example.)
What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?
"Out of sight out of mind" mentality that surrounds things taken for granted in an advanced society.
Who is your intended audience? Be specific.
All energy consumers.
What type of content might be included in your site? Be specific.
The general discussion and purpose will comprise the first page. This will be followed by short individual pages related to each of the current major sources (ranked by current usage) with their relative strengths and weaknesses. The last page will include the theoretical question and a form asking users to rank the sources in order of preference.
