Gouin-Web110-Milestone: Three

Site Map & Wireframe

Sitemap:

Create a sitemap using a Google Docs or any other appropriate tool, draw a flowchart (storyboard) of your web site that shows the hierarchy of pages and relationships between the pages.

Wireframe:

Use an appropriate tool to create a wireframe for a sample page on your web site that clearly shows the logo, navigation, content, and footer areas.
