M8 Recipe: Chicken Pad Thai

Ingredients
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 teaspoons Asian chili paste
1/4 teaspoon fish sauce (optional)
2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
8 oz rice noodles
3 tablespoons peanut oil
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon minced ginger
6 ounces chicken breast, cut into thin strips
3-4 pieces lime wedges
1 sprig, chopped finely fresh cilantro leaves
1/4 cup chopped peanuts

Preparation

  1. Put all the wet ingredients and the brown sugar in a food processor (top 7 ingredients.)
  2. Blend until smooth and uniform. This will form the sauce.
  3. Soak the rice noodles in warm water until softened. Then drain.
  4. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat and add the peanut oil.
  5. When the oil is hot, add the garlic and ginger and let cook for about 30-45 seconds.
  6. Next put in the chicken and stir fry.
  7. Cook for about 2 minutes or until slightly browned.
  8. Add the noodles and toss to coat.
  9. Add the sauce and toss the mixture to coat evenly.
  10. Cook for about 2 more minutes to warm mixture.
  11. Plate dish.
  12. Garnishes. Squeeze on lime juice. Add cilantro and peanuts.
  13. Enjoy!

Preparation time: 20-30 minutes || Serves: 1-2

This is a combination of many different chef's ideas that I rolled into one quick version, minus the fluff of side vegetables and ornamental items. Part Rachel Ray, part Cooking with Ming from PBS and part others.
